Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the nothern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at time this data was gathered.
